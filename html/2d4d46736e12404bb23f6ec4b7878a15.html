Objective of the Experiment

To study the Analog to digital and digital to analog conversion of sinusoidal signal.

Overview

In this experiment you can choose the number of bits ( from 6 ,8,10,12 bits) ,sample(generally 300 and above) and reference voltage from the numeric control and observe the change in the output of A/D and D/A .You can also check the Resolution of A/D from numeric indicator.
