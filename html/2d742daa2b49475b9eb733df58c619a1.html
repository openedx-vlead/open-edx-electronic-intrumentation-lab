Follow the steps as given below

a. Set the various circuit parameters as per requirement. The standard values appear by default.

b. The amount of magnetic field coupling can be controlled by either specifying the coefficient of mutual inductance M (Option 1) or by specifying the Magnetic Flux Density B (Option 2).

c. Click ‘Run’ button to initiate experiment and produce one time values. To vary values continuously and observe changes, click the ‘Run in Loop’ button.

d. Repeat experiment with different values.

e. End the experiment by clicking the ‘Stop’ button.
