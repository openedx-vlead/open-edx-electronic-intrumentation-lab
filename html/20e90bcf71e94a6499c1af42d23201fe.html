Objective of the Experiment

To study the functions of oscilloscope.

Overview

In This Experiment you can choose the signal like (sinusoidal, triangle, sawtooth, square wave) from the signal generator and Observed the display in the oscilloscope. In addition to this experiment you can also vary the knob of V/div, Time/div and triggering knob like positive/negative and X, Y position respectively and observed the difference in the display.
